Terms & Privacy

Policy Overview

This platform is designed for secure legal service communication between clients, lawyers, and administrators.

Data Usage

  • User account data is used only for authentication and service delivery.
  • Case details are processed only for legal request workflows.
  • Activity logs are maintained for audit and security monitoring.

Privacy Statement

  • Passwords are never stored in plaintext and are hashed using bcrypt.
  • Sensitive case descriptions are encrypted using AES-256 before storage.
  • Role-based access control restricts unauthorized data access.
  • Session and security controls protect user accounts.

User Responsibilities

  • Use strong passwords and do not share OTPs.
  • Log out after completing your session.
  • Report suspicious activity immediately.

Service and Compliance

By using this service, users agree to secure handling of legal information and policy-compliant usage of the platform.
